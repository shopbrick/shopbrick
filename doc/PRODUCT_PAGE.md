## ✨ Included Features

### 🛒 Built-in Shopping Cart

Customers can:

* add products to cart
* adjust quantity
* continue shopping
* checkout later

The cart is fully frontend-based and works without a backend server.

---

## 💳 Stripe & PayPal Checkout

ShopBrick supports:

* Stripe Checkout
* PayPal Checkout

Customers can instantly purchase products using trusted payment providers.

Orders are sent directly to Stripe and PayPal — no custom payment backend required.

---

## 🌍 Currency Switching

Users can switch between supported currencies directly from the navbar.

Exchange rates are generated locally using ExchangeRate API integration.

---

## 🌐 Country Selector

Configure countries where your shop operates.

Country switching can be used for:

* localization
* shipping rules
* pricing strategy
* payment availability

---

## 🎨 Product Variants

Support for:

* colors
* sizes
* variant-specific pricing

Variants are configured using simple YAML files.

---

## 🖼️ Product Image Gallery

Each product can contain:

* main gallery images
* inline description images

Images are filesystem-based and easy to manage.

---

## ⚡ Static + Fast

Product pages are generated as static HTML files.

Benefits:

* extremely fast loading
* CDN friendly
* easy deployment
* no database queries
* minimal infrastructure costs

---

## 🔧 Fully Configurable

Customize:

* colors
* themes
* currencies
* product data
* shipping countries
* branding

All configuration lives in simple YAML/text files.

---

Just products, files, and deployment.
